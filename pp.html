PP/QM Consultant

At Absys Technologies, our SAP PP/QM (Production Planning/Quality Management) consulting services focus on streamlining production planning and enhancing quality management processes. We help organizations align manufacturing workflows, ensure product quality, and achieve greater operational efficiency through intelligent SAP integration.

  • Optimize production planning and shop floor execution
  • Implement and configure quality inspections and controls
  • Integrate seamlessly with SAP MM, WM, and PM modules

Critical Functions of a Successful SAP PP/QM Consultant:

  • Design and implement production planning strategies
  • Configure routings, BOMs, and work centers
  • Set up quality inspection plans and control processes
  • Collaborate with SAP MM, WM, and PM modules
  • Handle process integration and issue resolution
  • Train users and document manufacturing/quality flows
